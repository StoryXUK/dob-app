Open Your Doors to NHS and Social Care Heroes

DoingOurBit would not exist without our amazing partners.

The generosity of stakeholders, both Partners and Sponsors, from across the fitness, leisure, sports and wellbeing sectors is humbling. This support has helped us create a platform for the benefit of all of our NHS and Social Care heroes.

Our Providers

If you’re a club, gym, or fitness studio, you can make a difference by offering free spaces in your classes or taster gym sessions. Your generosity can help our healthcare heroes unwind, recharge, and focus on their own health.

By partnering with us, you’ll join a community committed to giving back to those who give so much.

Our Partners

DoingOurBit would not exist without our amazing partners.

The generosity of stakeholders, both Partners and Sponsors, from across the fitness, leisure, sports and wellbeing sectors is humbling. This support has helped us create a platform for the benefit of all of our NHS and Social Care heroes.

None of this would be possible without the individuals and organisations that give their time and skills to host the activities available. If you are an instructor, studio or gym manager, and would like to support DoingOurBit and the thousands of NHS and Social Care workers, please click below to learn how.
